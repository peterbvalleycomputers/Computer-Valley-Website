Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our ISP services

What's included in installation?

All fibre installations include the ONT (modem), professional trenching or aerial cable routing, and basic indoor cabling. We'll get you online with a free router loan while you decide on your own equipment.

How long does installation take?

Typical fibre installations are completed within 5-7 working days from order confirmation. Wireless setups can often be done within 48 hours if line of sight is clear. We'll keep you updated every step of the way.

Are there contracts or cancellation fees?

We offer month-to-month packages with no long-term contracts. Cancel anytime with 30 days' notice. If you've received subsidized installation, a small equipment return fee may apply within the first 12 months.

What if I have connection issues?

Our local support team is available 24/7 via WhatsApp and phone. Most issues are resolved remotely within minutes. For on-site support, we typically reach you within 4-8 hours during business hours.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Absolutely! Speed upgrades are instant and can be done via a simple phone call or WhatsApp message. Downgrades take effect from the next billing cycle. No technician visit required for speed changes.
